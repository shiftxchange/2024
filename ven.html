Middlesex University Mauritius, situated in the lively and scenic touristic haven of Flic en Flac in collaboration with STADIO Higher Education and Business Mauritius is pleased to invite you to the International Conference ShiftXchange: A Practice-Based Approach to Rethinking Leadership in the Digital Era. Flic en Flac a sublime location along the west coast of Mauritius is one of the longest beaches on the island. The Flic en Flac beach is a great spot for embarking on a diving or snorkeling trip to explore the blue lagoon and coral reef.
