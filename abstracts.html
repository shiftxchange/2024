STADIO Higher Education, based in South Africa, in collaboration with Middlesex University Mauritius and Business Mauritius, is proud to announce the International Conference, ShiftXchange 2024: A Practice-Based Approach to Rethinking Leadership in the Digital Era. The conference is focused on the theme of collaborative solutions in the evolving digital landscape and aims to promote knowledge sharing, highlight challenges, and showcase best practices. The Call for Abstracts is currently open and we welcome contributions from academics, educators, researchers, business professionals, practitioners, innovators, leaders, and organizations with valuable insights and experiences to share.
All submissions will undergo a peer review process led by experts in the Scientific Committee. Authors will be notified of paper acceptance via email following the review process.

Download Call for Abstracts
Sub-Themes of the conference include:
  • Ethical and sustainable leadership
  • Leading in the financial sector
  • Leadership driving responsible technology
ShiftXchange 2024 Features:
  • 3 International Workshops during Day 1
  • Conference Presentations during 3 full days
  • 5 Plenary Sessions, including 2 Keynote Addresses
  • Events for Speed Dating and Networking, including Gala Dinner and Excursion

Important Dates

Due date for Abstracts: 30-Apr-24
Notification of acceptance: 30-May-24
Revised abstract submission (where applicable): 15-June-24
Early bird registration: 15-June-24
Final registration: 01-Aug-24
Due date for submission of full articles: 30-Sep-24

  • Using Avatars and AI to Promote Learning and Teaching
  • AI and Digital Technology – Sharing Good Practices
